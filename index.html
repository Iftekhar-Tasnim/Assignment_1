Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving creative solutions to build a better, smarter, and more sustainable future for all.

Sustainability

Commitment to environmental preservation, renewable energy, and sustainable growth.

Infrastructure

Building world-class infrastructure to support economic development and social progress.

Empowerment

Fostering opportunities for individuals to achieve their full potential and make a difference.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Each of us has much more hidden potential than we realize. Human beings are not born to suffer the misery of poverty. Poverty is not created by the poor themselves, but by the systems, structures, and mindsets that govern society. We must focus on creating an inclusive economy, where every individual has access to the resources and opportunities needed to succeed. Microcredit has shown that, given the slightest opportunity, the poorest can unleash their talents and work towards their own goals with dignity. A world free from poverty is not just a dream—it’s a human right and a possibility."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
